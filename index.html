About

A Montana Life,
Built from the Ground Up

I grew up here. I worked as a contractor, building beautiful kitchens and bathrooms across this valley — always dreaming of my own little piece of paradise someday. For years, that dream felt just out of reach.

I went on active duty at 37 and served this country with honor. When the time came to decide where to settle, my wife and I knew — Montana. We found a little cabin on the river in 2018. We could never afford to buy that same place today. That's not right. And it's why I'm running.

I remember a Montana where we didn't know — or care — what our neighbor's political party was. We just lived here, together. I want that Montana back. And I believe most of us do.

The Issues

What I'm Fighting For

These aren't liberal or conservative priorities — they're Montana priorities. Roads, healthcare, housing, and good schools belong to all of us.

🏡

Affordable Housing

Montana now has among the least affordable housing markets in the country. Median home prices have nearly doubled since 2018. Families who built this state are being priced out of it. That has to stop.

  • Fund the housing trust established in 2025 session
  • Streamline permitting for workforce housing
  • Protect renters with fair notice and anti-gouging measures
  • Expand homebuyer assistance for working families
🏥

Healthcare Access

Too many Montanans ration medications, skip appointments, or go bankrupt from a single health emergency. With federal ACA subsidies at risk, the state must step up to protect coverage.

  • Protect and strengthen Medicaid expansion
  • Support state-level reinsurance to lower premiums
  • Expand rural telehealth and clinic access
  • Prescription drug transparency and cost controls
💼

Economic Opportunity

Hard work should pay off in Montana. Wages aren't keeping pace with the cost of living, and too many families are making impossible choices every month.

  • Support small businesses and local contractors
  • Invest in apprenticeships and trade training
  • Fight for fair wages that reflect real costs
  • Oppose tax giveaways that blow budget holes
🤝

Bipartisan Leadership

In the last session, nine Republican senators were sanctioned and marginalized for having the courage to work across the aisle. That's not governance — that's tribalism. I'll fight for legislation on merit, not party loyalty.

  • Find common ground on shared priorities
  • Resist the politics of outrage and division
  • Hold leadership accountable to voters, not donors
  • Represent the middle — where most Montanans live
🌲

Public Lands & Environment

Montana's land, water, and wildlife aren't just beautiful — they're our economy. Hunting, fishing, ranching, and outdoor tourism depend on stewardship, not extraction.

  • Protect public access to public lands
  • Support sustainable water management
  • Oppose federal land transfers that limit access
  • Invest in wildfire prevention and recovery
🎓

Education & Infrastructure

Strong schools and reliable roads are the foundation of every Montana community. Both have been underfunded for too long while the bill gets passed to our kids.

  • Implement and fund the STARS Act teacher pay
  • Invest in rural broadband infrastructure
  • Fix bridges and roads before they fail
  • Fully fund Native American schools
